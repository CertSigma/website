Brand Rationale

Why "CertSigma"?

Sigma (Σ) is mathematics' symbol for the sum of all parts — precision, completeness, and rigour. In engineering, Six Sigma is the gold standard for zero-defect quality. We chose it because security certifications must be exhaustive, not approximate.

Cert signals our core service — certification audits. But it also evokes "certainty." Clients come to us because they want to be certain their security posture is real, not performative.

Together, CertSigma communicates: the complete sum of security certainty — a firm that delivers precision-grade outcomes, every time.

Why these colours?

Navy Anchor evokes institutional trust — the deep, considered authority of a CPA firm. Sigma Blue is modern, digital, and directional — it pulls the eye to actions. Audit Gold signals premium quality and certification achievement. Warm Parchment softens the palette, preventing the clinical coldness common in security brands. Together they say: rigorous but approachable, expert but not intimidating.

What we do

Why Outfit + DM Sans?

Outfit is a modern geometric sans-serif with clean, complete letterforms — every character is fully resolved and confident, including the G, which is often a tell for lower-quality display fonts. Its weight range (400–900) gives us expressive headlines without sacrificing legibility at smaller sizes. DM Sans is calm, highly legible, and designed for digital interfaces. The combination creates warmth and authority — professional enough for enterprise, modern enough for SaaS.

Voice & Tone

CertSigma speaks with precision

Authoritative

We don't hedge. We know compliance. Speak with confidence and specificity — no "we try to" or "we aim to".

Direct

Short sentences. Active voice. Tell clients what we do and why it matters — not what compliance is in general.

Precise

Use specific numbers, timelines, and outcomes. "3 weeks" beats "fast". "zero findings" beats "successful".

Warm

Not cold or corporate. Compliance is stressful — we are the reassuring expert in the room, not a bureaucrat.

Logo Usage Rules
Do

Use the Σ icon at minimum 28px height

Below this size the Sigma letterform loses its distinction and becomes ambiguous.

Don't

Stretch or distort the logo

The lockup uses a fixed ratio. Do not change aspect ratio, skew, or rotate the wordmark.

Do

Maintain clear space equal to the badge height on all sides

The logo needs breathing room. Never crowd it with other elements or edges.

Don't

Place the primary logo on coloured backgrounds without switching to the reversed version

Use the white/reversed version on any background darker than 50% lightness.

Do

Use the standalone Σ icon for app icons, favicons, and small contexts

The icon mark is designed to work in square containers at any size from 16px up.

Don't

Recreate the logo using a generic Sigma character

The Sigma badge uses a specific weight, size, and border-radius combination. Always use the provided asset.
